How "Duncan's Dating Life" Came To Be

Duncan and I first met in our Writing 150 class last semester and I was lucky enough to have him in this IS class with me this semester. Like any other good looking and capable young man attending BYU, Duncan is constantly searching for his future wife and that journey comes with many ups and downs. With permission given me by Duncan himself, I set out to create a simple website that highlights Duncan's best features and gives the wonderful and currently available bachlorettes of BYU a chance to get to know him a little bit better!
